One of the Methods to Solve Math Problems

2019-08-29
  1. Solve problems by finding that one constant that doesnt change, this concept is known as Invariance.
  2. You can find this by “playing around with the problem” and see what changes and what doesnt.
  3. Then you build you further solution on this invariant as youve successfully narrowed down possibilities.
  4. Do this iteratively.
