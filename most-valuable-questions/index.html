Most valueable questions

  1. Purpose: why are we doing this
  2. Questions - what are the best questions to ask
  3. Assumptions - can we safely assume them?
  4. Perspective: are the perspectives/ frame of references used of people valid? are we drawing insights from most informed people
  5. Information - is the data tying back
  6. Concepts - are we aligned on idea/ meanings
  7. Conclusions - assigning meaning to information

Logical fallacies

  1. Post hoc, ergo propter hoc - After this, therefore, because of this
  2. Ad hominem - attach person instead of argument
  3. straw man - replace original argument with exaggerated or distorted one
  4. Hasty generalisation - sample size is small

Planning fallacies

  1. overly - optimistic
  2. not considering earlier data

Framing

  1. decide to use positive or negative frame
  2. “gain” frmaing for clear message; “loss” framing for uncertain outcome
  3. match emotional frames to enhance +ve/ -ve framing
